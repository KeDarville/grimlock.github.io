Grim Park

Grimlock Schools

All three of Grimlock's schools were built on the same grounds, allowing the transition from school to school to be as seemless as possible for Grimlock's youngest residents.

Grocer's Lane

Grocer's Lane

Grocer's lane is Grimlock's shopping district. Equipt with grocery stores, resturants, boutiques, and more, it's a must visit when passing through our town!

Grimlock Reginal Hospital

Grim Lumberyards

The Grim Lumberyards

The Grim Lumberyards are what originally stood, and the town formed around it. The Grim family has been operating the yards since 1847, but there was a pause from 1974 to around 1990. From then on the lumberyards have been booming with production!

Nathan's Pier

Nathan's Pier

Named after the towns' founder, Nathan Grim, the pier is a relatively new edition to Grimlock's shoreline. In the summer it's a popular place for families to spend the day escaping the summer heat with the cooler ocean air.

Mountain Top Mall

Mountain Top Mall

With many stores to choose from, Mountain Top Mall is the one stop shop for most Grimlock locals.

Grimlock Hiking Trails

Grimlock Hiking Trails

The trails are one of the most popular destinations for Grimlock residents to meet and enjoy each other's company. The Jackalope trail is the most popular in the summer since it runs along the water's edge.
